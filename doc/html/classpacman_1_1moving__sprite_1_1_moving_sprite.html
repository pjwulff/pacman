def pacman.moving_sprite.MovingSprite.update (   self)

Update this sprite over the span of time of one frame.

This moves the sprite between two vertices in the graph. When it reaches a vertex it calls the protected _new_direction method to pick a new direction. This method must be overridden by subclasses.
